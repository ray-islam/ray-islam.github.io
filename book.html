Book Introduction

About the Book

Generative AI, Cybersecurity, and Ethics equips readers with essential skills to navigate the evolving AI and cybersecurity landscape. While generative AI advances threat detection and response, it also introduces challenges such as deepfakes, data privacy concerns, accountability, and ethical risk.

Through real-world examples and accessible chapters, the book explores machine learning, data privacy, ethical oversight, human-centered AI design, financial and legal impacts, regulatory challenges, and interdisciplinary approaches to safeguarding the digital future.
