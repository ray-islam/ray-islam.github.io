About

Dr. Ray Islam (Mohammad Rubyet Islam) is an AI scientist, author, educator, and technology strategist specializing in artificial intelligence, machine learning, responsible AI, and enterprise transformation. He is the author of *Generative AI, Cybersecurity, and Ethics* (Wiley) and has held strategic leadership roles across Fortune 500 and Fortune Global 500 organizations. Bridging academia and industry, Dr. Islam has served as both a professor and enterprise leader, translating cutting-edge research into practical business outcomes. He has led multimillion-dollar initiatives across finance, insurance, government, healthcare, manufacturing, retail, and defense, working with cross-functional teams across three continents. With five degrees earned across five countries, Dr. Islam brings a uniquely global perspective to innovation, leadership, and emerging technologies. His work spans research, education, enterprise strategy, and public engagement, with a focus on building trustworthy AI systems that create value for organizations and society.
